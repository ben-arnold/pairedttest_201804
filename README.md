# pairedttest_201804
A short lesson on the paired t test

## Summary

This repository includes slides that introduce the paired t test.  
It also includes an R notebook with examples, drawing upon the WASH Benefits Bangladesh trial as an example.  

Details of the trial have been previously published (open access):

Luby SP, Rahman M, Arnold BF, Unicomb L, Ashraf S, Winch PJ, et al. Effects of water quality, sanitation, handwashing, and nutritional interventions on diarrhoea and child growth in rural Bangladesh: a cluster randomised controlled trial. Lancet Glob Health. 2018;6: e302â€“e315.
